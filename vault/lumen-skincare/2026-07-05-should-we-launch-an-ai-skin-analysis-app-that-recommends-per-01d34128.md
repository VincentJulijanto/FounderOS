## Decision
Should we launch an AI skin-analysis app that recommends personalised skincare routines, or stay focused on selling our current skincare products through DTC channels?

### Context
Lumen Skincare has grown through online sales of cleansers, serums, and moisturisers, but customer retention is starting to flatten. Competitors are using quizzes, skin tracking, influencer communities, and personalised bundles to make customers feel like they are getting a tailored skincare journey rather than just buying products.

The board needs to pressure-test whether an AI skin-analysis app would meaningfully increase retention and average order value, or whether it would distract the team from improving the core product line. The decision should consider development cost, customer trust, privacy risks, accuracy of recommendations, brand differentiation, repeat purchase rates, and whether customers actually want tech inside a skincare brand.
